Quest Singing Competition

Discovering worship talents for the kingdom

Welcome to the Quest

This competition is designed to discover and nurture worship talents while maintaining godly values and excellence in music ministry.

Important Tips

  • This competition won't determine your success in life - it's just a tool toward achieving your dreams
  • Everyone is a winner in life's competition if they focus on God's will
  • Winning requires a positive mindset and right attitude

Competition Rules

  1. This is a Christian competition - no indecent dressing, vulgar or abusive language
  2. No loitering, littering, noise making, and all phones must be silent during auditions
  3. No swapping number tags or having someone continue on your behalf
  4. Late contestants will be disqualified
  5. Contestants must prepare their own song choice for first four stages

Additional Notes

  • Minimum of 5 contestants evicted at each stage
  • Auditions run for 4 consecutive Sundays (Sept 14 - Oct 5)
  • Only 10 finalists will perform at Sounds of the Spirit concert in November
